## Overview

Valence is an AI-powered platform that transforms how companies handle Mergers & Acquisitions due diligence. By combining intelligent document analysis, automated risk assessment, and real-time collaboration features, Valence eliminates delays and ensures no critical risks are overlooked during high-stakes transactions.

### The Challenge

M&A due diligence has traditionally been a bottleneck that slows deal timelines:
- Manual document reviews take weeks or months
- Teams working in silos miss important red flags
- Risk assessment relies on static checklists that lack real-world context
- Follow-up delays compound as stakeholders juggle multiple priorities

### The Solution

Valence streamlines the entire due diligence process by:
1. Automatically ingesting and analyzing documents from your data room
2. Identifying financial, legal, and valuation risks with detailed impact analysis
3. Coordinating findings across specialized analysis streams in real-time
4. Delivering clear, executive-ready recommendations for deal decisions

## How It Works

Valence coordinates five specialized AI agents that work together to analyze your transaction:

**Document Triager**
- First point of contact for all incoming documents
- Classifies documents by type and relevance
- Extracts key metadata and prepares findings for specialist review
- Routes analysis to appropriate teams

**Financial Forensic Agent**
- Deep dives into financial documents and statements
- Calculates key metrics and identifies anomalies
- Produces preliminary valuation ranges based on industry standards
- Flags concerns for further investigation

**Legal Compliance Analyst**
- Reviews contracts and legal agreements
- Identifies high-risk clauses (liability caps, intellectual property ambiguities, regulatory gaps)
- Assigns severity ratings to each risk
- Quantifies financial exposure in dollars

**Risk Synthesis Agent**
- Consolidates findings from all specialized agents
- Creates a unified risk assessment across Financial, Legal, and Valuation dimensions
- Calculates an overall risk score
- Prioritizes the top issues requiring resolution

**Executive Arbitrator**
- Reviews the complete analysis and risk matrix
- Produces a concise executive summary in plain English
- Issues a final recommendation: GO, CONDITIONAL GO, or NO-GO
- Flags findings requiring legal counsel or executive sign-off

All agents work through a shared memory system, ensuring each specialist can access complete context from previous analyses and collaborate seamlessly.

## Key Features

**Automated Document Processing**
- Instantly processes documents uploaded to your data room
- Intelligently categorizes financial, legal, IP, tax, and compliance materials
- Extracts structured data from documents for analysis

**AI-Powered Risk Analysis**
- Multiple specialized agents analyze your transaction from different angles
- Each agent understands the full transaction context
- Financial exposure is calculated in dollars for every identified risk

**Real-Time Collaboration**
- All agent communications stored in a shared memory system
- Complete conversation history available for reference
- Asynchronous workflow allows flexible analysis timing

**Deal Tracking & Insights**
- Real-time progress dashboard showing completion by analysis type
- Risk heatmaps across Financial, Legal, and Valuation categories
- Automated GO/NO-GO recommendations with escalation alerts for high-risk deals

## Use Cases

**Scenario 1: Financial Due Diligence**
Your finance team uploads quarterly revenue reports and balance sheets. Valence automatically calculates key financial ratios, identifies anomalies against industry benchmarks, and produces a preliminary valuation range—all within minutes.

**Scenario 2: Legal Risk Review**
Vendor and customer contracts are uploaded for analysis. Valence scans for high-risk clauses, quantifies financial exposure, and surfaces critical issues that might otherwise be missed in manual review.

**Scenario 3: Complete Deal Assessment**
Multiple document types are submitted for comprehensive review. Valence orchestrates sequential analysis across financial, legal, and valuation perspectives, then delivers a unified executive recommendation with clear GO/NO-GO guidance.

## Technology Stack

Valence is built on modern, proven technologies:

- **Workflow Automation:** n8n for orchestration and document routing
- **AI Agents:** Specialized language models for contextual analysis
- **Shared Memory:** Band.ai for agent coordination and collaboration
- **Document Storage:** Firestore for secure input and output management
- **Dashboard:** Real-time interface for transaction tracking and insights
- **Infrastructure:** React, TypeScript, Node.js, PostgreSQL, and industry-standard observability tools
